About Andean Pink Salt

A century ago it was discovered by primitive inhabitants, a layer of pink salt rock of captivating pink color due to the hight content of iron in their composition, located in the foothills of the southem part of the mountain range of the Bolivia Andes. Originated by geological processes. 300 million years ago. Where the sea was evaporated, and where these high content of iron, magnesium, calcium and salt residues were covered and protected from contamination.
